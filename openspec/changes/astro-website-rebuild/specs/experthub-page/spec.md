## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: ExpertHub hero
The ASUS ExpertHub page SHALL display a hero with headline "About ASUS ExpertHub" and a subtitle.

#### Scenario: Hero renders
- **WHEN** viewing `/en/experthub`
- **THEN** the hero section is displayed

### Requirement: ExpertHub marketplace section
The page SHALL include a marketplace section describing Arova Nexus availability on ASUS ExpertHub.

#### Scenario: Marketplace section renders
- **WHEN** viewing the experthub page
- **THEN** a marketplace-related section is visible

### Requirement: ExpertHub documents section
The page SHALL include a documents section with relevant resources and materials.

#### Scenario: Documents section renders
- **WHEN** viewing the experthub page
- **THEN** a documents section is visible

### Requirement: ExpertHub CTA
The page SHALL end with a CTA section.

#### Scenario: CTA renders
- **WHEN** scrolling to the bottom
- **THEN** a CTA section is displayed
